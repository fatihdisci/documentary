# Vanished Earth — channel identity

- **Channel name:** Vanished Earth
- **Handle:** @vanishedearthdocs
- **Channel URL:** https://www.youtube.com/@vanishedearthdocs
- **Audience:** Global English-language audience
- **Channel focus:** Evidence-based short documentaries about extinct animals, with one species per video.

## Official channel description

Vanished Earth tells the true stories of extinct animals — how they lived, what changed, and what their disappearance still teaches us.

From the dodo of Mauritius to the thylacine of Tasmania, each documentary follows one lost species through science, history, and the places it once called home.

Extinction is not just the end of a story. It is a warning for the living.

## Permanent visual-identity direction

The permanent channel identity must represent extinct animals globally, rather than the dodo alone. Use a species-neutral natural-history motif such as a fossil footprint transforming into a small planet, in charcoal, bone ivory and dark teal. Keep the mark simple enough to read at profile-icon size.
