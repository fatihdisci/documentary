# Global English YouTube Publishing Schedule

Timezone: `Europe/Istanbul` (Türkiye saati, UTC+3).

For every completed long-form documentary, publish the long video first and release its three supporting Shorts on this sequence:

| Relative time | Local time | Content |
|---|---:|---|
| Day 0 | 20:00 | Long-form documentary |
| Day 0 | 23:00 | Short 1 — strongest extinction or mystery hook |
| Day 1 | 02:00 | Short 2 — discovery, setting, or species hook |
| Day 1 | 06:00 | Short 3 — ecological angle, legacy, or secondary hook |

The next documentary is published at 20:00 on Day 1 and follows the same pattern.

## Notifications

YouTube limits viewers to three upload/live notifications from the same channel per rolling 24 hours. Keep notifications enabled for the long video and the two strongest Shorts. The remaining Short can be published without a subscriber notification if necessary.

## Upload requirements

- Viewer-facing metadata is English.
- Upload videos as private with a scheduled `publishAt` time.
- Include title, description, tags, category `Education`, default language `en`, and `selfDeclaredMadeForKids: false` unless the individual video is specifically made for children.
- Before retrying an interrupted upload, check the existing channel videos to avoid duplicates.
